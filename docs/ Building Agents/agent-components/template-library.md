If you find yourself recreating the same blocks over and over again, you can save time by turning them into templates.

Templates let you reuse sequences of blocks—whether it’s a single block with a few steps, or a full multi-block flow. Once saved to your library, you can drag them onto the canvas like any other step.

<br />

## Saving a template to the library

You can save any block (or group of blocks) as a template. Here's how:

* Select the blocks you want to save
* Right-click and choose **save to library**
* Give your template a name and choose a colour (optional)

<br />

## Using templates saved in your library

Once you've added at least one template to your project's library, you'll see a **Library** option underneath the other steps on the sidebar. When you hover over this option you’ll see a list of saved templates. Just drag one onto the canvas to use it. All blocks and paths inside the template will appear as part of your flow.

<Video src="https://yz5du1veb1.ufs.sh/f/9fKud4NeF5NSSrD38eVc0MDCZ1sln7dGipWvU8Vqz6TrtHw9" />

<br />

## Deleting saved templates

To delete a template saved in your library, right click the template in the library and select **delete**.
