You can **Log Out** of Voiceflow by navigating to the main dashboard. If you are on an assistant, you can do this easily by clicking the Voiceflow logo and hitting **Back to dashboard**.

![](https://files.readme.io/f2033ae-image.png)

Once on the main workspace dashboard you can then navigate to your **Account Name** located on the top right corner and selecting **Logout**.
