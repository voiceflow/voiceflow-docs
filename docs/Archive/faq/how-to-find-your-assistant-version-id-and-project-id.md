For some integrations, you might need to share your Assistant **project ID** or **version ID**.

Those IDs are available in the **Settings section** of your Assistant (you can use keyboard shortcut by pressing **5**).

![](https://files.readme.io/38fda30-image.png)

First, be sure to be on the **General** tab.

![](https://files.readme.io/fa7361a-image.png)

And scroll down to the **Metadata** section

![](https://files.readme.io/ae87a8d-image.png)

Now you can simply click on the **Copy** button to copy the **Project ID** or the **Version ID** to your clipboard.
